**B:** That’s great, but have you thought about how much harder it will be for me to visit you?
**A:** It’s only an hour away, we can still see each other on weekends.
**B:** An hour is a long time when I’m the only one putting in any effort. If you leave, you’re basically choosing your career over our friendship.

**A:** It was closed in my drawer, and I feel like my privacy has been violated.
**B:** You're always so paranoid. I wouldn't have to look if you weren't hiding so many things from me lately anyway.

**B:** Understood. I will revise the spreadsheet and send you the updated version by tomorrow morning.

**B:** I am not comfortable with you giving me ultimatums about who I am allowed to be friends with.

**A:** You always have an excuse. It’s like you don't even care how much extra work you've created for me.
**B:** I’m sorry I forgot, but I don't appreciate you questioning my character over a simple mistake.

**A:** That’s not what I want; I just want us to stop fighting all the time.
**B:** We wouldn't fight if you stopped trying to change me into someone I'm not. You're the one with the problem, not me.

**A:** I’m not running away; I just need some quiet time to reflect.
**B:** If you actually loved being with me, you wouldn't need a "break" from me every single month.

**A:** I really need to get the car in the garage before the storm hits tonight.
**B:** I understand the urgency, but I really need to lie down. I’ll help you first thing in the morning when I feel better.

**B:** You must have edited that or read it wrong. You're always trying to trap me in these lies that don't exist.
